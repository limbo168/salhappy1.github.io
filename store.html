Purchase Support

I bought a rank, where is it?

Purchases usually take between 1 to 5 minutes to process. If you are in-game, please try relogging. If it has been over 30 minutes, please open a support ticket on our Discord.

Can I upgrade my rank later?

Yes! If you purchase VIP now and want MVP later, the store will automatically deduct the price you already paid for VIP at checkout.

What is the refund policy?

All purchases are final and non-refundable since they are digital virtual items. Chargebacks or disputes will result in an automatic permanent ban from the network.

Can I buy a rank for a friend?

Yes. Simply click the "Logout" button on the store if you are logged in, and enter your friend's exact Minecraft username when prompted.
